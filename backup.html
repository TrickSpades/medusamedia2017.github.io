About

Medusa Media is a multi-purpose agency designed to create tactical and effective media.

Based in Los Angeles, CA, Medusa Media offers digital solutions to small and large and businesses with services including: Logo Design, Photography, Videography, and Web Development. We strive to create timeless products that will last.
